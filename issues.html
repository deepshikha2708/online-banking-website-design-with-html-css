Section 2: Common Issues

  • I forgot my online banking password. How can I reset it?

    You can typically reset your password through the bank's website or mobile app. Follow the password reset instructions, which may involve security questions or sending a reset link to your email.

  • I see unauthorized transactions on my account. What should I do?

    Report the unauthorized transactions to your bank immediately. They will investigate and, if necessary, issue a refund while taking measures to prevent further fraud.

  • I want to close my bank account. How can I do it?

    Visit a local branch or contact your bank's customer service to request the account closure process. Ensure all outstanding transactions and fees are settled.

  • I can't access my account online.

    Contact your bank's customer support to resolve login issues, which may involve password resets, account verifications, or addressing technical difficulties.

  • I haven't received my account statement.

    Check your online banking for electronic statements or contact your bank to request a statement or confirm your mailing address.
